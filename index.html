How to create a new
issue on Linear app

This interactive tour will guide you through the process of creating a new issue on Linear, a project management tool.

1

Navigate to the CloudWorks team and click on “All Issues”

2

Click on the 'Create Issue' button located on the top right of the Linear toolbar. This will open the issue creation modal.
